MURDER ON THE ORIENT EXPRESS

A universal classic. One of Hercule Poirot's most famous cases.The most popular novel featuring the legendary detective Hercule Poirot. In a remote part of the former Yugoslavia, in the dead of night, a fierce snowstorm blocks the railway line where the Orient Express is traveling. On board, arriving from exotic Istanbul, is detective Hercule Poirot, who suddenly finds himself facing one of the most perplexing cases of his career: in the neighboring compartment, Samuel E. Ratchett has been murdered in his sleep, despite no clues pointing to a specific motive. Poirot will use the situation to investigate the other occupants of the carriage, who, by all appearances, should be the only possible perpetrators of the crime. One victim, twelve suspects, and a brilliant mind in search of the truth.

El alquimista

The adaptation of Paulo Coelho's masterpiece. This is the story of Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd who dreams of traveling the world and finding the most fabulous treasure ever discovered. He journeys from his home in Spain to the markets of Tangier and from there to the pyramids of the Egyptian desert, where a magical encounter awaits him. Paulo Coelho's The Alchemist is a story with the power to inspire us to pursue our dreams.
